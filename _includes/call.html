CALL FOR PAPERS

The workshop will consist of contributed posters, and invited talks on a wide variety of methods and problems in this area. We invite paper submissions in the following categories to present at the workshop:

  • Knowledge base completion using multiple modalities
  • Using knowledge bases in NLP tasks
  • Vision and knowledge bases
  • Information extraction
  • Optimization challenges in multimodal scenarios
  • Benchmark datasets and evaluation methods

Submissions should be in the form of extended abstracts of no more than 1 page (not including references or supplementary materials). All submissions must use the AKBC template.
